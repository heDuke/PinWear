# PinWear

PinWear is a third-party Wear OS client based on the official Pinterest API, dedicated to bringing a native and modern Pinterest browsing experience to your smartwatch. 

*PinWear 是基于 Pinterest 官方 API 开发的第三方 Wear OS 客户端，致力于为您的智能手表带来原生、现代的 Pinterest 浏览体验。*

---

## English Version

### 🎯 MVP Features
The current Minimum Viable Product (MVP) goals include:
- **Native Wear OS Experience**: Designed with Compose for Wear OS and Material 3 for circular screens.
- **Browse Your Boards**: View a list of your personal Pinterest Boards.
- **Browse Your Pins**: View the Pins saved within your Boards.
- **Pin Details**: See specific Pin images and descriptions right on your wrist.
- **Open in Official App**: Seamlessly open Pinterest official pages on your connected phone or browser.

### 🛠 Tech Stack
- **Kotlin** & **Coroutines** for robust and asynchronous logic.
- **Jetpack Compose for Wear OS** & **Material 3** for modern UI.
- **Retrofit** & **Kotlin Serialization** for network communications.

## 中文版 (Chinese Version)

### 🎯 MVP 目标功能
当前的最小可行性产品 (MVP) 目标包括：
- **Wear OS 原生体验**：使用 Compose for Wear OS 和 Material 3 专为圆形表盘设计的现代交互。
- **浏览个人画板 (Boards)**：支持查看当前登录用户自己的画板列表。
- **浏览图钉 (Pins)**：支持查看画板内的图钉列表。
- **查看 Pin 详情**：在手表上直接查看具体的图钉图片和描述信息。
- **打开官方页面**：支持从手表触发，在手机端或浏览器中打开 Pinterest 官方页面。

### 🛠 技术栈
- **Kotlin** & **Coroutines** 构建健壮的异步逻辑。
- **Jetpack Compose for Wear OS** & **Material 3** 构建现代 UI。
- **Retrofit** & **Kotlin Serialization** 处理网络通信。
